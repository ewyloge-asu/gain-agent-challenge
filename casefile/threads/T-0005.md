corpus before publishing. Drugmaker money reported in Sen. Tammy Baldwin's honor:
**$500 (2023) + $12,500 (2024) → $168,500 (2025)** — dominated by **two $80,000 Takeda
payments to the National Organization for Rare Disorders, made in her honor** (item dates
2025-03-25 and 2025-10-20, filing UUIDs above). Her pharma/drug-price press releases:
**48 from Jan 2023–May 2025, then one passing mention June 2025–Mar 2026**, while overall
output stayed high (192 releases in the quiet window).

self-reported. The hand count ($167,500; 37 strictly-critical releases) and the pipeline
count ($168,500; 48 pharma-related) differ by method — documented, not reconciled away, in
`traces/outputs/13_baldwin_pharma_verification.json`.
