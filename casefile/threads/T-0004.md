**Re-verified this session** (previously a prior-work result reached independently by all
three solo attempts): House 2025 re-ingested into this store — 108,518 filings, 98.3%
bridged to Senate engagements via senateID + name fallback. Across **36,643 comparable
engagement-periods, zero** differed in reported income by more than $100K. A clean dead
end, reported on purpose as a credibility signal.

**Methods catch:** the naive join first showed 19 six-figure "gaps" — all were quarterly
amendment artifacts (original filing in one chamber compared against the amendment in the
other; e.g. Mercury/Prvo Plinarsko Q2: $1.2M originally in both chambers, amended to $120K
in both). Amendment-aware de-dup on both sides (latest filing per engagement-period — the
same discipline that corrected Loc Nation) collapsed all 19 to zero. Folded into
`xref.py mismatch` so the tool can't repeat the mistake.
