Hypothesis: registered-lobbyist LD-203 money pools on the members who control whether
legislation moves (committee chairs / ranking members + floor leadership).

Evidence (2025, this run): the resolved gatekeeper leaderboard (honoree→member, 81.8%
coverage after seeding members from the congress-legislators roster and resolving
campaign-committee honorees like "Guthrie for Congress") puts **Brett Guthrie
(E&C chair) #1 at $694K** from lobbyist filers (177 registrants), then Jason Smith
(Ways & Means), Mark Warner, Scalise, Johnson, Tillis, Cole, Carey, Thune, Jeffries —
overwhelmingly committee chairs/ranking members and floor leaders.

The discretionary signal is **bill sponsorship** (what a member chooses to push), not floor
votes (which are ~97–99% party-line, so donor-alignment at the vote level is confounded by
party). Documented ≠ causation.
